How to use RoomDesigner

  1. Upload your room image – pick a photo of your living room or bedroom.
  2. Choose wall paint – select a colour chip; cost is added automatically.
  3. Add furniture – use the right panel and click Add to room.
  4. Move items – drag with your mouse or finger.
  5. Resize – drag the blue circle.
  6. Rotate – drag the green circle.
  7. Mirror – select an item and click the ⇋ button.
  8. Save – click Save Design to store the layout.
  9. View later – open My Designs to see and reopen saved layouts.

If you clear your browser data, your saved designs will be removed (they are stored only on this device).
